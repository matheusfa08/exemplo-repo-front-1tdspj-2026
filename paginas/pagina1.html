About the book

It's a manga series about a post-apocaliptic world where the objects gain a soul and are deeply connected to the people that own them.

The manga is very interesting and even it has a unique art style. An art style from the streets, and a very intense mood that makes it stand out.

The story follows Rudo, a boy that lives in the margins of society. Unfortunately, he is pointed as the assasin of his mentor, and so, he is throwned out the margins of the city to the ABYSS...

In the abyss, he finds a lot of strange characters, strange creatures and he has to fight for his life, and learn about the world he now lives in.
