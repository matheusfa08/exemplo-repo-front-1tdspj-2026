About the book

It's a BRAZILIAN manga series about two ramdom guys that meet in the street and are chased by a group of people with the power of the death will of their beloved ones (They also have that power).

The manga is very interesting and has a WACKY and funny story, it's a true mark in the brazilian manga industry, and it's a great way to spend some time!

I've spent a lot of time reading this manga and I'M NOT ADDICTED! I SWEAR! I JUST WANT TO THEORIZE IF NOAH TRUE POWER IS TO KNOW HOW TO END EVERY FIGHT!
