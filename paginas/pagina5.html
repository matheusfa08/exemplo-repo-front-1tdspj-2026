About the book

It's a BRAZILIAN novel about a secret organization that protects the world from paranormal threats, and the protagonists of that story are very strange and might be somehow connected to the paranormal world BY IT'S GUTS.

The novel showed up as a way to explore more aspects of the world of Ordem Paranormal (A RPG focused on the paranormal theme).

It's good that they didn't stop there and continued to develop the novels further. The creator, Rafael "Cellbit" Lange, has done an excellent job of building the world and characters.

If I had to give you all a hint, it would be to stay up to date with the seasons of the RPG show in youtube. Cause it evolved so much, and it's so pretty... It makes me cry, even after watching it multiple times.
